Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about becoming a TiffinLoop chef.

What are the requirements to become a chef?

You need to have a passion for cooking, access to a clean kitchen, knowledge of food safety practices, and the ability to prepare and deliver meals according to schedule. All chefs must also pass a background check and food safety verification.

How much can I earn as a TiffinLoop chef?

Earnings vary based on your menu pricing, order volume, and how often you choose to cook. Many of our active chefs earn between $500-$2000 per month, with some top performers earning significantly more.

Do I need professional cooking experience?

No, professional experience is not required. Many of our successful chefs are home cooks who have learned from family recipes or developed their skills through passion and practice.

How does the payment process work?

Customers pay through the TiffinLoop platform when they place an order. We handle all payment processing and transfer your earnings to your account weekly, minus our service fee.
